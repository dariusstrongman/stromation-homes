Plano Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

Every part of Plano has its own personality. Here's a quick rundown of the areas our agents know best.

Legacy West / Shops at Legacy

This is where a lot of the new energy in Plano is. Corporate headquarters, upscale restaurants, walkable shopping. Homes near Legacy tend to hold their value really well, and buyers in this area are usually relocating for work. If you're selling here, you probably have more leverage than you think.

West Plano

Bigger lots, newer builds, and some of the best schools in the district. West Plano is where families with older kids tend to land. Prices are higher here, but you get a lot for your money compared to Park Cities or Southlake. The 75093 zip code is especially popular.

Preston Hollow Area

The southern end of Plano near Preston Road has a great mix of established neighborhoods and updated homes. It is close to Richardson and North Dallas, so commutes are reasonable in most directions. This area is a solid pick for people who want to be closer to the city without actually living in Dallas.

East Plano / Downtown Plano

If you want more house for less money, East Plano is worth a look. Downtown Plano has a walkable strip with restaurants and shops, and the DART station makes it easy to get around without a car. Prices here are noticeably lower than the west side, making it a good entry point for first-time buyers.
