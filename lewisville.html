What a lot of people love about Lewisville is that you get a high quality of life at a price point that's more affordable than neighboring Flower Mound and Highland Village. Lewisville Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

Lewisville has a mix of master-planned communities and established neighborhoods, each with its own character and price range. Here's what our agents see on the ground.

Castle Hills

Castle Hills is one of the most popular master-planned communities in the DFW area. It spans parts of Lewisville and The Colony, with amenities like pools, parks, trails, and a town center with shopping and dining. Homes here range from the mid $300Ks to over $600K depending on the section. The community feel is strong, and it's a top pick for families who want newer construction and a walkable neighborhood.

Valley Ridge

Valley Ridge is a well-established neighborhood in the northern part of Lewisville with mature trees, larger lots, and a quieter feel. Homes here are typically in the $350K to $500K range and offer more space than what you'd find closer to town. It's a solid choice for families who want good schools, a neighborhood feel, and easy access to the tollway. Resale values have stayed strong in this area.

Lake Vista

Lake Vista offers some of the best proximity to Lewisville Lake you can find in the city. If you want lake access, parks, and a more relaxed pace of life, this area is worth a look. Homes tend to be priced from the upper $200Ks to around $400K. It's popular with buyers who love the outdoors and want that lake lifestyle without paying lakefront prices. Weekend boating and fishing are right out the back door.

Old Town Lewisville

Old Town is where Lewisville's history and character come together. The area around Main Street has seen a revitalization in recent years, with local restaurants, shops, and community events drawing people in. Homes in Old Town are generally more affordable, often in the $250K to $350K range, and you get a lot of charm for the money. If you want walkability, local flavor, and a sense of community, Old Town delivers.

Common Questions About Lewisville Real Estate

How much is my Lewisville home worth right now?

It depends on your neighborhood and the condition of your home. The citywide median is around $355K, but that covers everything from starter homes in Old Town to larger builds in Castle Hills. Is Lewisville more affordable than Flower Mound and Highland Village?

Generally yes. Lewisville offers a similar quality of life, the same school district (LISD), and comparable access to the tollway and I-35E, but at a lower median price point. Many buyers who start looking in Flower Mound or Highland Village end up choosing Lewisville once they see how much more home they can get for their budget. What are the best neighborhoods in Lewisville for families?

Castle Hills and Valley Ridge are the most popular picks for families, thanks to newer homes, community amenities, and proximity to top-rated LISD schools. Lake Vista is also a great option if your family loves the outdoors. Our agents can help you narrow down the best fit based on your budget, school preferences, and lifestyle.
