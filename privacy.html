Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you fill out a form on our Site, contact us, or otherwise interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Contact information: first name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Property information: property address, city, state, ZIP code, property type
  • Transaction details: price range or estimated home value, buying or selling timeline, whether you are a buyer or seller
  • Communication preferences: SMS opt-in consent, email preferences
  • Correspondence: the content of emails and text messages you exchange with us, including conversations with our team

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URL, click behavior
  • UTM and source tracking parameters: campaign source, medium, campaign name, and page URL used to reach our Site
  • Cookies and similar technologies: as described in Section 7 below

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from publicly available sources, including county appraisal district records and property data, which we use to provide relevant outreach for homeowners who may benefit from our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Lead qualification: To understand your real estate needs and determine whether we can match you with a suitable agent
  • Agent matching: To connect you with a licensed, local real estate agent in the DFW area who serves your city, price range, and property type
  • Communication: To send you welcome emails, qualification emails, agent referral information, follow-up messages, and responses to your inquiries via email or SMS
  • Service improvement: To analyze how visitors use our Site, improve our matching algorithms, and enhance the user experience
  • Marketing: To send you relevant real estate information, market updates, and promotional materials (with your consent or where permitted by law)
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Fraud prevention: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, spam, abuse, or security incidents

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

Matched Real Estate Agents

When you are qualified and matched with a real estate agent, we share your name, contact information (email and/or phone number), property details, price range, timeline, and intent type (buyer or seller) with the matched agent so they can assist you. This is the core purpose of our service. We only share your information with licensed real estate agents who have agreed to our referral terms.

Service Providers

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate our business. These providers process your data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect it:

  • Supabase (database hosting) -- stores your lead information securely in the cloud
  • OpenAI (artificial intelligence) -- processes email content to qualify leads and generate responses (see Section 6 on AI-Powered Communications)
  • Zoho Mail (email services) -- sends and receives emails on our behalf
  • Twilio (SMS messaging) -- sends and receives text messages when you opt in to SMS communications
  • Google Analytics (website analytics) -- collects anonymized usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with our Site

Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, or to investigate fraud or respond to a law enforcement request.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

4. SMS/Text Messaging Consent

Your consent is required before we send you any text messages. We will never send you SMS messages unless you explicitly opt in by checking the SMS consent checkbox on our forms.

How SMS Consent Works

  • Opt-in: By checking the SMS consent checkbox on our buyer or seller form, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Stromation Homes at the phone number you provide. This consent is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services.
  • Message types: You may receive text messages related to your real estate inquiry, including welcome messages, qualification questions, agent match notifications, and appointment reminders.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interactions with our service. You will typically receive no more than 5 messages per week.
  • Standard rates: Message and data rates may apply. Your carrier's standard messaging rates will apply to any messages you send or receive.

How to Opt Out of SMS

You may opt out of text messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message you receive from us
  • Emailing us at team@stromation.com with the subject line "SMS Opt-Out" and your phone number

After you opt out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive any further text messages from us unless you opt in again.

Quiet Hours

In compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), we do not send text messages outside of the hours of 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM Central Time, Monday through Sunday. If a message is triggered outside of these hours, it will be queued and sent during the next available window.

SMS Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your phone number may be shared with your matched real estate agent solely for the purpose of assisting you with your real estate transaction.

5. Email Communications

Transactional Emails

When you submit a form on our Site, you will receive transactional emails related to your inquiry, including:

  • A welcome email confirming your submission
  • Qualification emails to understand your needs
  • Agent match notifications with your assigned agent's contact information
  • Responses to questions or messages you send to us

Transactional emails are necessary to provide you with the service you requested and are not subject to marketing opt-out.

Marketing and Cold Outreach Emails

We may send informational or promotional emails if you have an existing relationship with us or if we have a lawful basis to contact you. All marketing emails comply with the CAN-SPAM Act and include:

  • A clear identification of the message as an advertisement (where applicable)
  • Our valid physical mailing address or a valid registered PO box
  • A clear and conspicuous opt-out mechanism
  • Honest and accurate subject lines

If you receive a cold outreach email from us, it is because we identified you as a homeowner who may benefit from our services based on publicly available property records. You may opt out of these communications at any time.

How to Unsubscribe from Emails

You may opt out of marketing and outreach emails by:

  • Replying STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE to any email you receive from us
  • Emailing us at team@stromation.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe"

We will process your opt-out request within 10 business days, as required by the CAN-SPAM Act.

6. AI-Powered Communications

Disclosure: Some email communications you receive from Stromation Homes may be generated or assisted by artificial intelligence (AI) technology.

We use AI-powered systems to help qualify leads, respond to inquiries, and facilitate the agent matching process. Specifically:

  • Email qualification: When you respond to our emails, your message content may be processed by an AI system (powered by OpenAI) to understand your needs, determine your readiness to buy or sell, and craft a helpful response.
  • Response generation: Some email replies you receive from our team email address (team@stromation.com) are generated by AI, though they are designed to be helpful, professional, and relevant to your inquiry.
  • Human oversight: Our team monitors AI-generated communications and may intervene at any time. All agent referral decisions are subject to review.
  • Data processing: The content of your email exchanges is transmitted to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's data usage policies apply to this processing. We do not use your conversations to train AI models.

If you prefer to communicate with a human representative instead of our AI system, please email team@stromation.com with the subject line "Human Only" and we will accommodate your request.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses the following cookies and tracking technologies:

Essential Cookies

We use localStorage to store UTM parameters (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign) that help us understand how you found our Site. These are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to third-party advertisers.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-B6Z6XV02RT) to collect anonymized data about how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track page views, session duration, traffic sources, and user behavior. This data helps us improve our Site and services. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Site.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Lead data: We retain your contact and property information for up to 3 years after your last interaction with us, or until you request deletion.
  • Conversation history: Email and SMS conversation logs are retained for up to 3 years for quality assurance, dispute resolution, and compliance purposes.
  • Analytics data: Anonymized website usage data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention settings (14 months).
  • Opted-out records: If you opt out of communications, we retain a record of your opt-out preference indefinitely to ensure we honor your request.

When your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out of communications: You may opt out of marketing emails, cold outreach emails, and SMS messages at any time (see Sections 4 and 5).
  • Data portability: You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at team@stromation.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond to your request within 30 days (or sooner if required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

Your California Privacy Rights

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources, the business purpose for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., completing a transaction, legal obligations).
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by the CPRA beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

Categories of Personal Information Collected

In the preceding 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, IP address
  • Customer records: Property address, city, ZIP code, price range, timeline
  • Internet activity: Browsing history on our Site, search queries, interactions with our Site
  • Geolocation data: Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Inferences: Lead score, qualification status, buyer/seller intent

How to Submit a California Privacy Request

California residents may submit a request by emailing team@stromation.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days, as required by law. You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf.

11. Texas Residents

If you are a Texas resident, the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), effective July 1, 2024, provides you with certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may confirm whether we are processing your personal data and access that data.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal data you have provided to us.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may obtain a copy of your personal data in a portable, readily usable format.
  • Right to Opt Out: You may opt out of the processing of your personal data for targeted advertising, the sale of personal data, or profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not engage in profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on Texas residents.

How to Submit a Texas Privacy Request

Texas residents may submit a request by emailing team@stromation.com with the subject line "Texas Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days. If we decline your request, you may appeal by emailing us with the subject line "Texas Privacy Appeal."

12. Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect it, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our Site is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure database hosting: Your information is stored in Supabase, which provides encrypted-at-rest database storage, row-level security policies, and regular backups.
  • Access controls: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only on a need-to-know basis.
  • Email security: Our email domains are protected by SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication to prevent spoofing and phishing.
  • API security: All integrations with third-party services use secure API keys and authentication tokens.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining industry-standard protections.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at team@stromation.com and we will promptly delete such information from our records.
