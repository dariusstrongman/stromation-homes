Why People Love Living in Wylie

Wylie Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

Wylie has a mix of established neighborhoods and brand-new master-planned communities. Each one has its own feel and price range. Here's what our agents see on the ground.

Birmingham Farms

Birmingham Farms is one of the most established neighborhoods in Wylie. You'll find mature trees, larger lots, and a real sense of community here. Homes range from the mid $300Ks to the low $400Ks, and the neighborhood is zoned for some of Wylie ISD's most popular schools. It's the kind of place where neighbors know each other and kids ride bikes in the street. If you want Wylie character without new construction, this is a great pick.

Alanis Ranch

Alanis Ranch is a newer master-planned community with modern floor plans and community amenities. Homes here tend to be in the $350K to $450K range, and builders are still active in some sections. The community pool, playground, and walking trails make it popular with young families. It's close to shopping along FM 544 and has easy access to the rest of Collin County. A strong option for buyers who want new or nearly new construction.

Inspiration

Inspiration is one of the largest master-planned communities in Wylie and it lives up to the name. Resort-style amenities, multiple pools, a fitness center, and miles of trails. Homes range from the high $300Ks into the $500Ks depending on the builder and lot size. The community is still growing, which means buyers have options across different price points. It's a popular choice for families who want the full community lifestyle experience.

Woodbridge

Woodbridge is a well-established community in Wylie that offers solid value. Homes are generally in the $325K to $400K range, with good lot sizes and a neighborhood feel that's hard to beat. The community has parks, walking paths, and is well-located for Wylie ISD schools. Resale homes here move quickly because buyers know the neighborhood and trust the value. It's a reliable choice for both buyers and sellers in the Wylie market.

Wylie is one of the fastest-growing cities in Collin County, and home values have been trending upward.
