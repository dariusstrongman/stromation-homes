Allen Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

Allen has a variety of neighborhoods, each with its own character and price range. Here's what our agents see on the ground.

Twin Creeks

Twin Creeks is one of Allen's most established and sought-after master-planned communities. Built around the Twin Creeks Golf Club, it offers mature trees, winding streets, and a real sense of neighborhood. Homes here range from the mid $400Ks to over $800K depending on lot size and updates. It is a favorite for families who want space, good schools, and a community pool and clubhouse. Sellers in Twin Creeks benefit from consistent buyer demand.

Montgomery Farm

Montgomery Farm is anchored by Watters Creek, one of the best mixed-use developments in the area with restaurants, shops, and green space. The homes here are newer, well-designed, and walkable to amenities that most suburbs don't offer. Prices typically start in the high $400Ks and go up from there. If you want the convenience of urban-style living in a suburban setting, Montgomery Farm is hard to beat.

Ridgeview

Ridgeview is a newer development in Allen that has attracted a lot of attention from young families and move-up buyers. The homes are modern with open floor plans and energy-efficient features. Prices generally range from the low $400Ks to the mid $500Ks. The neighborhood is close to US-75, making commutes to Plano, Richardson, and downtown Dallas straightforward. It is a strong pick for buyers who want a newer home without the custom-build price tag.

Star Creek

Star Creek is one of Allen's premier newer communities, featuring larger lots and higher-end finishes. Homes here often start in the $500Ks and can go well above $700K. The neighborhood has excellent amenities including a resort-style pool, fitness center, and walking trails. It is zoned for some of the best schools in Allen ISD. Buyers looking for a move-in-ready home with upscale features consistently put Star Creek at the top of their list.
