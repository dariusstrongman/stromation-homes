The Colony Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

The Colony has a range of neighborhoods, each with its own character and price point. From lakeside luxury to established family communities, here's what our agents see on the ground.

Austin Waters

Austin Waters is one of The Colony's most popular master-planned communities. You'll find a mix of single-family homes with solid square footage, community pools, walking trails, and a neighborhood feel that families love. It's close to Grandscape and the Tollway, which makes commuting easy. Homes here offer strong value compared to what you'd pay in Frisco for similar size and quality, and the community stays in demand year-round.

Stewart Peninsula

Stewart Peninsula is where The Colony meets Lewisville Lake. This area offers some of the best waterfront and water-view properties in the entire DFW metroplex. Homes range from updated ranch-style houses to newer construction with lake access. If you want to be on or near the water without driving to a vacation house, Stewart Peninsula delivers. It's a unique lifestyle that's hard to find this close to Dallas.

Legends

Legends is a well-established neighborhood in The Colony with mature trees, larger lots, and a quiet suburban feel. It's a great fit for families who want space and don't need the newest construction. Homes here tend to be priced below the city median, making it one of the more affordable entry points into The Colony. The neighborhood is close to schools and shopping, and longtime residents tend to stay because the community is tight-knit.

Tribute Lakeside

Tribute is The Colony's premier lakeside community, built around a Tom Fazio-designed golf course with resort-style amenities. Homes here are on the higher end for The Colony, but you get a lot for it -- lake views, pools, fitness centers, and a community that feels like a destination. Tribute attracts move-up buyers and professionals who want upscale living without the Frisco or Southlake price tag. If you're selling in Tribute, demand stays strong.
