Rockwall Neighborhoods Worth Knowing

Rockwall may be smaller than Dallas, but each neighborhood has its own character, price range, and appeal. Here's what our agents see on the ground.

The Shores

The Shores is one of Rockwall's premier master-planned communities, built right along Lake Ray Hubbard. You get resort-style amenities, a private beach and pool complex, walking trails, and some of the best lake views in the metroplex. Homes here range from the mid-$400Ks up to well over $700K for larger lakefront lots. If you are selling in The Shores, demand stays consistently high because of the lifestyle and the Rockwall ISD schools.

Heath (Nearby)

Heath sits just east of Rockwall and shares a lot of the same lake lifestyle and school district appeal. The lots tend to be larger and the feel is more rural and spacious. You can find estate-style properties on acreage here, which is hard to come by this close to Dallas. Heath is popular with families who want more room to spread out while staying connected to Rockwall's downtown and amenities.

Chandlers Landing

Chandlers Landing is a well-established lakefront community with a country club, marina, and golf course. It has been a Rockwall landmark for decades and still attracts buyers who want waterfront living with all the amenities. Homes range from the $300Ks for updated condos and townhomes up to $600K+ for lakefront single-family homes. The marina and boat slips are a big draw for boating families.

Stone Creek

Stone Creek is a newer development that offers solid family homes at a more accessible price point than the lakefront communities. You get well-built homes, a community pool, parks, and easy access to Rockwall's shopping and restaurants. Prices typically range from the low $300Ks to the mid-$400Ks. It is a great option for first-time buyers and families who want to be in Rockwall ISD without the premium of lake-adjacent properties.
