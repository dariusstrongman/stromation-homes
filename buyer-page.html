Common Questions

What buyers ask us most

Do I have to pay for this?
No. Buyer's agents are paid by the seller (typically 2.5-3% of the sale price). You don't pay a dime.
What if I'm not pre-approved yet?
Totally fine. Your agent can connect you with lenders we trust and help you get pre-approved fast.
How long does it take to buy a home in DFW?
Most buyers close in 30-60 days. Finding the right place usually takes 2-8 weeks depending on what you're looking for.
What if I don't like the agent you match me with?
No worries at all. There's no contract or obligation. Just tell us and we'll match you with someone else.
What price range can I afford in DFW?
A general rule: your monthly housing payment should be no more than 28% of your gross monthly income. Your agent can help you determine your exact budget.
Will you spam me?
No way. You'll get one welcome email and an agent will reach out within 24 hours. That's it. No drip campaigns, no spam.
