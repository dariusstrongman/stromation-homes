Common Questions

Do I have to pay for this?
Nope! Buyer's agents are paid by the seller (typically 2.5-3% of the sale price). You get expert help at zero cost.
What if I'm not pre-approved yet?
That's okay! Your agent can connect you with trusted lenders and help you get pre-approved quickly.
How long does it take to buy a home in DFW?
From start to finish, most buyers close in 30-60 days. Finding the right home can take 2-8 weeks depending on your criteria.
What if I don't like the agent you match me with?
No problem! There's no contract or obligation. If it's not a good fit, just let us know and we'll find you a better match.
What price range can I afford in DFW?
A general rule: your monthly housing payment (mortgage + taxes + insurance) should be no more than 28% of your gross monthly income. Your agent can help you determine your exact budget.
Will you spam me?
Never. We respect your inbox. You'll get one initial email, and an agent will reach out within 24 hours. That's it. No annoying drip campaigns.
